Uday Bhavan, located in Tilakwadi, Belagavi, Karnataka, is a well-established hotel and event venue offering both comfortable accommodations and facilities for hosting events. It features 37 rooms, including both air-conditioned and non-air-conditioned options, catering to various budgets. The hotel’s in-house restaurant is known for serving delicious South Indian vegetarian cuisine. Uday Bhavan provides a banquet hall with a capacity to accommodate up to 250 guests, making it suitable for weddings, receptions, and other social events. While it offers in-house catering and decoration services, outside catering is not permitted. The hotel is conveniently situated near the RPD Bus Stop and is easily accessible for guests. Room rates start at ₹2,500 per night, offering budget-friendly options.
