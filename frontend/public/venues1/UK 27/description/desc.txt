UK 27 The Fern is a luxurious 5-star hotel located in the heart of Belagavi, Karnataka, offering modern amenities and eco-friendly hospitality. The hotel features 90 well-appointed rooms, including Deluxe Rooms, Studio Rooms, and Suites, equipped with essential amenities like air conditioning, minibars, flat-screen TVs, and tea/coffee makers. Guests can enjoy a range of dining options at the hotel's two restaurants, Spices and Milange, and unwind at the stylish bar/lounge. The hotel also offers a rooftop swimming pool, fitness center, spa services, and a sun terrace with scenic views. With facilities such as free Wi-Fi, 24/7 room service, and on-site parking, it provides convenience and comfort for both leisure and business travelers. Located centrally, the hotel is close to local attractions like Belgaum Fort and Gokak Falls. Room rates typically start at ₹4,500 per night, and for reservations, guests can visit the hotel’s official website or contact the management directly.
