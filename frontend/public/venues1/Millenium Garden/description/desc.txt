Millennium Gardens, located in Tilakwadi, Belagavi, is a versatile event venue known for hosting a variety of functions, including weddings, cultural events, and social gatherings. The venue offers a spacious hall suitable for large-scale events, and its central location ensures easy accessibility for guests. In addition to serving as an event space, part of the Millennium Gardens complex has been developed to house a PVR multiplex and a Brand Factory outlet, enhancing its appeal as a multifunctional destination. The venue is also recognized for hosting cultural events, such as Navratri celebrations, adding to its reputation as a community hub.
