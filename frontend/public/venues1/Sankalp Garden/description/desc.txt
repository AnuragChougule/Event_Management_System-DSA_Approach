Sankalp Garden Multi Function Hall, located in Ramteerth Nagar, Lakshmipuri Layout, Auto Nagar, Belagavi, Karnataka, is a versatile venue suitable for various events such as weddings, receptions, birthday parties, and traditional ceremonies. The hall offers essential amenities including ample parking, uninterrupted water supply, and generator backup to ensure a smooth event experience. Its strategic location near the cricket stadium on Kanbargi Road makes it easily accessible for guests. The venue provides flexible packages at reasonable prices, catering to diverse event requirements. For bookings and inquiries, interested individuals can contact the management.
