Shivam Function Hall is a versatile event venue located on Dr. SPM Road in Shastri Nagar, Khasabag, Belagavi, Karnataka. Situated approximately 1.25 kilometers from the Belagavi railway station, it offers convenient access for guests. The hall is suitable for various events, including weddings, engagements, birthday parties, and cultural functions. It features essential amenities such as a stage, seating arrangements, lighting fixtures, and audio-visual provisions. The venue also provides housekeeping services and VIP seating options. Operating hours are from 10:00 AM to 6:30 PM on weekdays and from 11:00 AM to 6:30 PM on Sundays.
